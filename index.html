About Me

I am passionate in the field of surveying with a ‘no problems just solutions’ approach to each and every project. With almost 3 years in the surveying industry, I’m competent with a multitude of surveying disciplines and techniques, with a strong knowledge of 12d and Autocad software. During my time at my current employer, Cardno, I’ve developed a strong work ethic and a capacity to solve problems and continue to grow my experience as a surveyor. I have the capacity to see jobs through end-to-end, including preparing survey calculations, completing jobs efficiently and finally, completing plans with a high attention to detail.
